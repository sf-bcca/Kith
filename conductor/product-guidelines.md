# Product Guidelines

## Brand Voice & Prose Style
- **Warm and Personal:** Kith's communication should be welcoming and inclusive, emphasizing the emotional connection of family history and the importance of storytelling.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over Complexity:** High-density genealogy data must be presented with extreme clarity. Prioritize readability and intuitive navigation to ensure the platform is accessible to all age groups.
- **Visual Storytelling:** Leverage photos, documents, and interactive media to transform dry records into a rich, engaging narrative.
- **Consistency and Familiarity:** Utilize standard UI patterns and Material Design principles to provide a predictable and comfortable experience for users.

## Privacy & Security
- **Privacy-First Messaging:** Build trust by being transparent about data protection. Clearly communicate privacy settings and ensure users feel in control of their family's information.
